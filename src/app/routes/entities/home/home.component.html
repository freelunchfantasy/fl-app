Free Lunch takes your league's rosters and matchups to run thousands of simulations of the rest of your league's season. See the place your team is most likely to finish!
Simulate a trade between two teams in the league to see how it affects the league's outlook! Free Lunch will identify how many wins each team in the league might gain/lose if the trade is executed.
Trades may be a zero-sum game for the league, but it doesn't have to be for the teams involved! Now you can propose trades to your leaguemates with proof that the trade will help both of your teams.
